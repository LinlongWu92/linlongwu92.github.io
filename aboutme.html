About Me

I am currently a Research Scientist at SnT, University of Luxembourg, where I have been since Nov. 2020. Prior to this, I worked as a Research Engineer at Alibaba Cloud from Nov. 2018 to Oct. 2020. I received my Ph.D. in Electronic and Computer Engineering from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in Nov. 2018. My research focuses on nonlinear optimization, signal processing and machine learning with applications in wireless sensing and communications.
